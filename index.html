Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Most people who are healthy and over the age of 18 can donate blood. Donors should weigh at least 50kg and be free from certain medical conditions. Our medical professionals will conduct a brief health check before each donation to ensure eligibility.

How often can I donate blood?

Whole blood donors can typically donate once every 3 months (4 times per year). This waiting period allows your body to replenish the donated blood. Plasma donors may be able to donate more frequently, approximately every 28 days.

Is donating blood safe?

Yes, donating blood is very safe. All equipment used is sterile and disposed of after a single use. The donation process is conducted by trained healthcare professionals who follow strict safety protocols. Most donors experience minimal discomfort and recover quickly.

How is my data protected?

We take data privacy very seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only share your information with verified medical professionals when there's a need for your blood type. You can review our full privacy policy for more details.
